Döner

A Döner being prepared in a local shop.

Super popular around the world, really tasty and can be adapted to anyones taste!

Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Meat, usually Chicken/Lamb
  • Salad
  • Sauces
  • Onions
Steps
  1. Put everything into the bread
  2. Eat
  3. Never want to eat something else again
